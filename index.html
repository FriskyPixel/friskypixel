About

I just want to do it all! Although my professional career has mostly involved video editing, motion graphic design, and 3D/CGI creation, I still have a passion for my Computer Science roots. That's probably why I'm currently wielding dual responsibilities as both Senior Video Editor and Web Developer at my current job. I have helped produce Owner Information videos for a variety of auto industry clients in over 20 languages, as well as manage the supporting team behind the projects - all while creating the company's website, managing our internal WordPress intranet site, and creating landing pages for our various products and services.

I am a strong believer in life-long learning and I constantly jump in on projects that are outside of my normal responsibilities, always learning something new in the process. I've created databases for internal tracking of our employee training, researched and tested development of 360° video and VR development using Unity, set up customer-facing webinars, and worked with our Marketing and Sales departments to create a variety of e-mail marketing campaigns. There's never a time where I don't strive to either learn something new or more fully master my primary job.
